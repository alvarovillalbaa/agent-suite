Use skill: **competitors** — `skills/competitors-intelligence/SKILL.md`.

1. **Set the scan scope** – Ask for the market, customer segment, or named competitors if the request is vague.
2. **Confirm the window** – Default to the last 7 days unless the user specifies another period.
3. **Run the scan** – Follow the competitors skill to identify new entrants, feature moves, and why they matter.
4. **Add strategic response** – For each finding, include the recommended response or implication.
5. **Deliver** – Output a concise findings report with evidence and the next strategic question.
