Use skill: **blog-articles** — `skills/blog-articles/SKILL.md`.

1. **Gather the source** – Ask for notes, research, transcript, rough draft, or topic plus audience and goal if those are missing.
2. **Clarify the article shape** – Capture whether SEO matters, what action the reader should take, and any tone constraints.
3. **Draft the article** – Follow `blog-articles` to produce angle options, structure, and the full article draft.
4. **Tighten for usage** – Include title options, slug, and meta description when relevant.
5. **Deliver** – Output the article and suggest `social-pack` if the user wants distribution variants.
