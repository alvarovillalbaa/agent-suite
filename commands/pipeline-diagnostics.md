Use skill: **fundraising** — `skills/fundraising/SKILL.md`, but focus on Process Diagnostics and the Speed-Up Plan.

1. **Gather pipeline context** – Ask for CRM or funnel data, current stage, target close date, and any obvious bottlenecks.
2. **Analyze the process** – Identify stage counts, drop-offs, delays, and weak spots in the funnel.
3. **Produce the speed-up plan** – Turn the diagnosis into a critical path, time savers, and accountability matrix.
4. **Keep the scope narrow** – Do not expand into deck messaging or materials audit unless the user asks.
5. **Deliver** – Output the diagnostics, speed-up plan, and the single highest-leverage next action.
