# Output Checklist

Before delivering a visualizer artifact, confirm:

- The output mode matches the task.
- The title explains what the artifact is about.
- The first screen tells the reader why they should care.
- The page is readable on laptop and phone widths.
- The visual direction is intentional and not generic.
- Tables are legible and not wider than necessary.
- Diagrams are not overloaded with tiny labels.
- Each section earns its place.
- The recommendation, takeaway, or decision is visible without deep scrolling.
- Claims are supported by the source material.
- The final file path is reported clearly.
