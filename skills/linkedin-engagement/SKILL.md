# LinkedIn Engagement Skill

Draft LinkedIn comments, connection messages, and engagement replies that are valuable, concise, and on-brand.

## Purpose

- **Comments** on others’ posts: add insight or question, avoid generic praise; 1–3 sentences.
- **Connection requests / DMs:** personalized, reason to connect, clear next step; no spammy tone.
- **Follow-up messages** in sequences: reference prior touchpoint; one ask or value add.

## Inputs

- Context: target post or profile summary; relationship (cold/warm); goal (visibility, meeting, reply).
- Optional: tone (formal/casual), length limit, “do not” list (e.g. no pitch in first message).

## Outputs

- Draft comment or message ready to paste (with optional 1–2 variants).
- Short note on why this angle (e.g. ties to their post, mutual interest).

## When to use

- User asks for a LinkedIn comment, connection message, or engagement reply.
- Social-media-manager or sales-manager invokes this for outreach or community building.

## Validation checklist

- Personalized (references their content or role).
- No generic flattery; adds value or clear ask.
- Length appropriate (comment: short; DM: 2–4 lines unless narrative).
- No unsolicited pitch in first touch unless user explicitly requests it.
