# Content brief structure

Standard sections for a content or campaign brief:

- **Objective:** Measurable or observable goal (e.g. "Increase sign-ups from blog by 10%")
- **Audience:** Role, segment, or persona (e.g. "HR directors at 50–200 person companies")
- **Key messages:** 1–3 messages; one differentiator or proof point each
- **Format / channel:** Blog, video, LinkedIn, X, newsletter, etc.; word count or duration
- **Tone:** Professional, casual, technical, etc.
- **Timeline:** Publish date or campaign window
- **Success criteria:** How success will be measured
- **Next steps / owner:** Who approves and who executes
