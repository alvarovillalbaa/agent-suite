# Investor messaging frameworks

Use these when producing **Investor-Ready Messaging** (core narrative, tailored hooks, FAQ rebuttals).

## Core narrative (≤50 words)

- **Why now:** Market timing, regulatory shift, tech enablement, or window of opportunity.
- **Why us:** Team, traction, defensibility, or unfair advantage in 1–2 concrete claims.
- Avoid jargon; use one clear metric or proof point if possible.

## Tailored hooks (1-line per investor)

- Match hook to fund thesis, portfolio, or recent blog/social.
- One variant per named investor; cite what you know (sector, stage, geo).
- Prefer specificity over generic “we’re the best.”

## FAQ rebuttals

- Top 5 questions investors actually ask at your stage (e.g. unit economics, burn, competition, cap table).
- Each answer: 1–2 sentences + one data cite (slide, metric, source).
- Mark estimates as `(est.)`; cite deck/source inline `[Source:Slide5]`.
