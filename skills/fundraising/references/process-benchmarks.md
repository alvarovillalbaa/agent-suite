# Process benchmarks (fundraising)

Use when producing **Process Diagnostics** and **Speed-Up Plan**. Compare pipeline and stage metrics to these reference ranges (top-quartile SaaS at similar stage).

## Stage duration (days, indicative)

| Stage | Seed / Pre-A | Series A | Series B+ |
|-------|--------------|----------|-----------|
| Outreach to first meeting | 7–14 | 10–21 | 14–28 |
| First meeting to partner meeting | 14–21 | 21–35 | 28–42 |
| Partner meeting to term sheet | 14–28 | 21–42 | 28–56 |
| Term sheet to close | 21–45 | 30–60 | 45–90 |

## Pipeline health

- **Win rate (meeting → term sheet):** often 10–25% for institutional; track by source.
- **Drop-off by stage:** identify the stage with highest % drop and root-cause (e.g. missing metrics, slow follow-up).
- **Benchmarks:** flag if days-in-stage is >1.5× the ranges above; suggest time savers in Speed-Up Plan.

*These are indicative; adjust for vertical and geography. Mark as (est.) when not from user data.*
