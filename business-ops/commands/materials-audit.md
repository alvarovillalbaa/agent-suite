Use skill: **fundraising** — `skills/fundraising/SKILL.md`, but focus on the Materials Upgrade Report.

1. **Gather the materials** – Ask for the deck, model, data room summary, and any stage context if it is missing.
2. **Review the materials** – Score the assets and identify the most important gaps.
3. **Prioritize the fixes** – Produce the upgrade report with severity, next action, owner, and due date where possible.
4. **Keep the scope narrow** – Do not expand into investor messaging or pipeline diagnostics unless the user asks.
5. **Deliver** – Output the audit and the prioritized fix list.
