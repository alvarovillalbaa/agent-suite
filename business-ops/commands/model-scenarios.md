Use skill: **financial-modeling** — `skills/financial-modeling/SKILL.md`.

1. **Gather the model context** – Ask for the business type, stage, key drivers, historicals, or existing spreadsheet.
2. **Confirm the scenarios** – Define base, upside, downside, or any named scenarios the user wants.
3. **Build or review the model** – Produce assumptions, formula logic, and scenario outputs.
4. **Call out risks** – Highlight inconsistent assumptions, formula risk, or model blind spots.
5. **Deliver** – Output the scenario view and the assumptions table.
