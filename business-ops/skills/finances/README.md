# Finances

Unified finance skill covering expense operations, bill monitoring,
reconciliation, month-end close management, CFO briefings and scenario work,
tax-document readiness, and fundraising support.

## Use this for

- categorizing expenses from receipts, bank notifications, or transaction logs
- monitoring budgets, spending drift, recurring bills, and autopay/manual-payment risk
- reconciling statements, ledgers, receipts, and audit trails
- planning and tracking close calendars, dependencies, and blockers
- building or reviewing P&L, cash flow, runway, scenario, or cap-table models
- turning QuickBooks or ERP exports into executive CFO briefings
- organizing tax documents into year/category checklists and advisor-ready evidence packs
- organizing evidence and finance artifacts in shared storage such as Drive
- turning decks, models, CRM notes, and investor feedback into fundraising materials or diagnostics

## Install

```bash
npx -y skills add ./business-ops/skills/finances
mkdir -p ~/.codex/skills
cp -R business-ops/skills/finances ~/.codex/skills/
```
