A simulated run of the full launch system, showing the transformation from weak to production-ready at each step.

---

## Context

**Product:** An AI website builder  
**Category:** No-code / website tools  
**Target community:** Freelancers and small business owners  
**Raise:** Seed / product launch

---

## Step 1 — Hidden Outcome Recursion

**Feature list:**
1. Builds a website in 30 seconds
2. AI-generated copy
3. Built-in payments
4. Custom domain support
5. Mobile-optimized by default

**"So what?" recursion on Feature 1:**

> "Builds a website in 30 seconds"
> → So what? You can launch fast
> → So what? You can start your business today
> → So what? You don't need savings, a developer, or months of runway
> → So what? You can replace your 9-5 income without quitting yet

**Hidden outcome:** "Replace your 9-5 income"

**Positioning test:** This describes a life change (income replacement), not a feature (fast website builder). Passes.

## Step 3 — Controversy Engineering

**Target community:** Small business owners, freelancers, and aspiring entrepreneurs

**Activating nerve:** The belief that starting a business requires quitting your job and risking financial security

**What designers/critics will say:** "This is just another no-code toy. You can't build a real business with this."

**The plant line:** Already embedded — "replaces your 9-5 income" is the controversy.  
People who believe you can't replace income with an AI website builder will quote-tweet to argue. People who believe you can will quote-tweet to share with friends who need it.

Both groups feed the sourcing signal.

---

## Step 4 — Concept Borrowed

**YouTube research finding:** "How I [achieved life-changing outcome] using [simple mechanism]" is the highest-performing frame in the solopreneur and passive-income categories.

**Applied framing:** The mechanism (30-second AI builder) delivers the life-changing outcome (income replacement). Lead with the outcome; the mechanism is the proof.

## Step 6 — 25 Reply Drafts (48-Hour Window)

Sample replies for common comment types:

**"This is amazing, how does it work?"**
> The AI interviews you for 30 seconds, builds the site, writes your copy. No templates. It's built around what you actually do. Try it — completely free right now.

**"This is too good to be true"**
> I said the same thing. Then watched someone build their bakery website while I was on the call with them. 30 seconds. Live. Their first customer came through it that week.

**"Does this actually replace a developer?"**
> For most small businesses? Yes. For a funded startup with custom requirements? No. But for someone who needs to be online and take payments today? It's everything they need.

**"I've tried these before and they all suck"**
> That's fair. The difference is the AI writes the copy for you — no blank page, no templates. Build one right now and see if it changes your mind. Free, no card required.

**"How much does it cost?"**
> Free to start. Paid plans when you're ready to connect your custom domain. Build yours first — takes 30 seconds. [link]

**"What kind of businesses does this work for?"**
> We've seen it work for: freelancers, coaches, restaurants, boutiques, consultants, photographers. If you can describe what you do in one sentence, Durable can build around it.

**"I built websites for 20 years this will never replace me"**
> You're right — it won't replace someone who builds complex custom sites. It replaces the decision that a freelancer or small business owner makes when they go to Squarespace instead of hiring anyone. Different market entirely.
