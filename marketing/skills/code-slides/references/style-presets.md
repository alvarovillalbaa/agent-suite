# Style Presets

Use these presets when the user wants visual direction quickly, or use them as inputs for three preview variants.

## Rules

- Keep presets distinct in typography, color, and motion.
- Favor bold, intentional design over generic startup gradients.
- Use expressive fonts instead of system defaults when the delivery format permits web fonts.
- Preserve existing brand language if the repository already establishes one.

## Presets

### Bold Signal

- Vibe: confident, high-impact, presentation-first
- Best for: pitch decks, launches, keynote-style storytelling
- Typography: `Archivo Black` with `Space Grotesk`
- Color direction: dark base with a vivid orange or coral card focal point
- Motion: assertive staged reveals and number-led transitions

### Electric Studio

- Vibe: clean, professional, agency-polished
- Best for: product demos, client presentations, portfolio work
- Typography: `Manrope`
- Color direction: strong black/white contrast with a saturated blue accent
- Motion: restrained but crisp panel and quote transitions

### Dark Botanical

- Vibe: elegant, premium, atmospheric
- Best for: luxury products, storytelling decks, premium services
- Typography: high-contrast serif display with modern sans body
- Color direction: dark field with muted botanical greens and soft highlights
- Motion: slow fades, layered depth, floating abstract forms

### Notebook Tabs

- Vibe: editorial, organized, human
- Best for: reports, onboarding, structured walkthroughs
- Typography: `Fraunces` or similar editorial serif with practical sans body
- Color direction: warm paper base with tab accents
- Motion: tab changes, page shifts, understated reveals

### Pastel Geometry

- Vibe: friendly, approachable, modern
- Best for: product overviews, education, light brand narratives
- Typography: rounded or geometric sans pairing
- Color direction: soft pastels with clear contrast anchors
- Motion: pill, shape, and block transitions with playful timing

### Neon Cyber

- Vibe: futuristic, tech-heavy, electric
- Best for: developer tools, AI products, forward-looking demos
- Typography: sharp display sans with utilitarian mono accents
- Color direction: dark canvas with neon green, cyan, or acid accents
- Motion: scanline, glow, and terminal-inspired transitions

### Swiss Modern

- Vibe: precise, minimal, information-first
- Best for: corporate, data, strategy, or technical decks
- Typography: strong neo-grotesk sans with rigorous hierarchy
- Color direction: restrained monochrome with one disciplined accent
- Motion: subtle slides, grid-aware transitions, minimal ornament

### Paper and Ink

- Vibe: thoughtful, literary, narrative
- Best for: storytelling, founder narratives, reflective presentations
- Typography: serif-led display with editorial body text
- Color direction: off-white, charcoal, muted ink tones
- Motion: calm fades and line-drawing accents

## Preview Workflow

When the user is unsure, generate three lightweight previews that intentionally span different directions:

1. One safe direction aligned with the audience.
2. One bolder direction with stronger contrast or motion.
3. One alternative with a different typography family or layout logic.

Each preview should show:

- A title slide
- Heading/body hierarchy
- Background treatment
- Accent and navigation treatment
- Entry animation style
