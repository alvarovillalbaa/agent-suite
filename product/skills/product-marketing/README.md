# Product Marketing

Product marketing skill covering positioning, messaging, ICPs, product marketing context, landing pages, CRO, content strategy, and buyer psychology.

## Use this for

- positioning, messaging, and differentiation
- ICP and persona work
- product marketing context documents
- landing pages and CRO audits
- structured copy editing for existing marketing assets
- content-led marketing plans and audience-building strategy
- general lead magnet strategy, gating, and distribution
- survey-driven lead magnet strategy
- content strategy and brand voice

## Install

```bash
npx -y skills add ./product/skills/product-marketing
mkdir -p ~/.codex/skills
cp -R product/skills/product-marketing ~/.codex/skills/
```

Codex `$skill-installer` path:

```text
https://github.com/alvarovillalbaa/plugins/tree/main/product/skills/product-marketing
```

- `references/`
- `scripts/`
